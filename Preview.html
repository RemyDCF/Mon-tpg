Description
tpg offline is an application that allows you to travel easily with the Geneva public transport.
You can view upcoming departures, current events, plans, and even build itineraries.

You can also have stops and routes favorites, change theme and view the next departures without any network, free of charge.

This application uses open data provided by the Geneva public transport for departures and incidents and open data provided by opendata.ch for routes.

Whether or not you are in offline mode, tpg offline can in no case be responsible for delays and advances of transport, as well as modifying schedules or transportation.

Now, go on an adventure and have a good trip!

In this version, tpg offline has a brand new design, with an icon refreshed for more autumnal colors.

* iPhone X *

The application has been completely rewritten for this version. Yes, everything. Starting on new and more stable bases, many dependencies have been removed to allow a much better stability, a lighter app and especially much faster app to start and use.

Now, in addition to being Open Source, tpg offline now has a collaborative translation site. If you speak German, Italian, Spanish or even Japanese, you can participate in the translation of the application. Feel free to take a tour on https://www.transifex.com/remydcf/tpg-offline/.

Description
tpg offline est une application qui vous permet de voyager facilement avec les transports publics genevois.
Vous pouvez consulter les prochains départs, les incidents actuels, les plans et même construire des itinéraires.

Vous pouvez aussi avoir des arrêts et des itinéraires en favoris, changer de thème et consulter les prochains départs sans aucun réseau, et ce gratuitement.

Cette application utilise l'open data fournit par les transports publics genevois pour les départs et incidents et l'open data fournit par opendata.ch pour les itinéraires.

Que ce soit en mode offline ou non, tpg offline ne peut en aucun cas être responsable de retards et d'avances des transports, ainsi que des modification d'horaires ou de transports.

Maintenant, partez à l'aventure et bon voyage à Genève !
